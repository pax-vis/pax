Each horizontal line on the timeline represents one agreement. Agreements signed in the same year are stacked on top of one another. Hover over an agreement (a horizontal line) to view its details in the left sidebar. The number above each yearly group of agreements displays the total number of agreements signed in the corresponding year. Select filters in the left sidebar to view agreements and yearly totals for specific years, countries/entities, or codes.
